### 説明
Digest::SHA1を使ったUUID

### 使い方
    Digest::UUID.uuid_v5(UUIDの名前空間, 名前)

### 例
#### Digest::SHA1を使ったUUID
    Digest::UUID.uuid_v5("name_space", "sample")
    # "d7a9ae1a-b099-5318-8828-340cc10a1550"
